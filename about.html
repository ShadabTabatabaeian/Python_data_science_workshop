About the Instructor

My name is Shadab Tabatabaeian. I love statistics and programming. I also love teaching, and my goal is to show my students that stats and programming can be really fun when you apply them to answer questions that matter to you.

Currently, I'm a postdoctoral researcher at Georgetown University, working on the integration of AI technology in education. Before this position, I worked as a senior data scientist for the EdTech sector in San Diego.
